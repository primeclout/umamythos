How these stories are meant to travel

Our Philosophy

These stories have always been free — told around fires, under trees, between generations. Uma Mythos exists to continue that tradition in a new form. We believe stories grow when they travel.

Copyright

All stories, illustrations, and content on Uma Mythos are original works © 2026 Uma Mythos. The written narratives, visual artwork, and overall presentation are protected by copyright.

You're welcome to share these stories freely — that's what they're for. If you share, please credit Uma Mythos and link back to umamythos.com

What You Can Do

Share links to stories with friends, family, or anyone who might enjoy them. Post about Uma Mythos on social media. Recommend stories to others. Read them aloud to children. Use the share buttons we've provided. Quote brief passages with attribution.

What We Ask You Not To Do

Please don't reproduce entire stories or illustrations without permission. Don't remove our branding or attribution. Don't use our content for commercial purposes. Don't claim the work as your own.

For Educators & Non-Commercial Use

If you'd like to use Uma Mythos stories in a classroom, library, or other non-commercial educational setting, we're generally happy to grant permission. Please reach out and let us know how you'd like to use the stories.
